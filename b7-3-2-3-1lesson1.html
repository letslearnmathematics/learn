Describing Bearings

Bearings

Bearings are a way to describe the direction of one point relative to another. They are commonly used in navigation, geography, and surveying to provide accurate directional information.





Describing Bearings

A bearing is the angle measured in degrees in a clockwise direction from the north line to the line connecting two points. Bearings are always measured from the north and written as three digits for clarity. For example, a bearing of 45 degrees is written as 045Â°.
