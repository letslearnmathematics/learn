Section B

Question 1

  1. Simplify: \( \frac{3}{4} + \frac{1}{2} - \frac{2}{3} \)

    Solution

    Find LCM of denominators (4, 2, 3) = 12

    \( \frac{3}{4} = \frac{9}{12} \)
    \( \frac{1}{2} = \frac{6}{12} \)
    \( \frac{2}{3} = \frac{8}{12} \)

    \( \frac{9}{12} + \frac{6}{12} - \frac{8}{12} = \frac{7}{12} \)

    \(\therefore\) The simplified form is \( \frac{7}{12} \)


  2. If \( p = 3 \) and \( q = -2 \), evaluate \( p^2 - q^2 \)

    Solution

    \( p^2 - q^2 = (3)^2 - (-2)^2 \)

    \( = 9 - 4 = 5 \)

    \(\therefore\) The result is 5


  3. Factorize completely: \( 6x^2 - 3xy \)

    Solution

    \( 6x^2 - 3xy = 3x(2x - y) \)

    \(\therefore\) The complete factorization is \( 3x(2x - y) \)

Question 2

A rectangular garden measures 15m by 10m. A path of uniform width is built around the garden. If the area of the path is 74m², find the width of the path.

Solution

Let width of path = \( x \) meters

New dimensions: \( (15 + 2x) \)m by \( (10 + 2x) \)m

Area of garden + path = \( (15 + 2x)(10 + 2x) \)

Area of garden alone = \( 15 \times 10 = 150 \)m²

Area of path = \( (15 + 2x)(10 + 2x) - 150 = 74 \)

\( 150 + 50x + 4x^2 - 150 = 74 \)
\( 4x^2 + 50x - 74 = 0 \)
Divide by 2: \( 2x^2 + 25x - 37 = 0 \)

Using quadratic formula: \( x = \frac{-25 \pm \sqrt{625 + 296}}{4} \)

\( x = \frac{-25 \pm \sqrt{921}}{4} \)
\( x ≈ 1.37 \)m (discarding negative solution)

\(\therefore\) The width of the path is approximately 1.37 meters

Question 5

A trader bought 100 oranges at 5 for ₵1.20. 20 oranges got rotten and the remaining were sold at 4 for ₵2.00. Calculate the:

  1. Total cost price of the oranges

  2. Selling price of the remaining oranges

  3. Profit made

Solution

5(a): Cost price

Cost per orange = ₵1.20 ÷ 5 = ₵0.24

Total cost = 100 × ₵0.24 = ₵24.00

5(b): Selling price

Good oranges = 100 - 20 = 80

Selling price per orange = ₵2.00 ÷ 4 = ₵0.50

Total selling price = 80 × ₵0.50 = ₵40.00

5(c): Profit

Profit = Selling price - Cost price = ₵40.00 - ₵24.00 = ₵16.00

\(\therefore\) The profit made was ₵16.00
