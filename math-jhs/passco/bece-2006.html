  1. A trader sold 250 articles for ₵525,000.00 at a profit of 25%.

    \((i)\) Calculate the cost price of each article.

    \((ii)\) If the trader had wanted 45% profit on the cost price, how much should he have sold each of the articles?

  2. Find the simple interest on ₵880,000.00 for \(2\frac{1}{2}\) years at \(3\frac{1}{4}\%\) per annum.



Question 2


  1. The ratio of men to women in a village is \(12:25\). If there are 120 men,

    \((i)\) How many women are there?

    \((ii)\) What is the total number of men and women?

  2. A bag contains 70 pencils out of which 15 are green and 30 blue.

    \((i)\) How many pencils of other colours are in the bag?

    \((ii)\) A pencil is selected from the bag at random. What is the probability that it is blue?

  3. Solve \(\frac{1}{3}(x - 1) - \frac{1}{2}(x - 3) \leq 1\frac{1}{4}\) and illustrate your answer on the number line.
