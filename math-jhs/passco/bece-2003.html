    \(ξ\) = {1, 2, 3, 4, ..., 18};

    \(A\) = {Prime numbers} and

    \(B\) = {Odd numbers greater than 3}

  1. If \(A\) and \(B\) are subsets of the universal set, \(ξ\), list the members of \(A\) and \(B\).

  2. Find the set

    \((i)\) \(A \cap B\)
    \((ii)\) \(A \cup B\)

  3. \((i)\) Illustrate \(ξ\), \(A\) and \(B\) on a Venn diagram

    \((ii)\) Shade the region for prime factors of 18 on the Venn diagram.
