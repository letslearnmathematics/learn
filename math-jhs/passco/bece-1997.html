Question 1


  1. If \(p = 7, a = 16, b = -5\) and \(c = 3\),
    evaluate \(p^2 - \frac{(a - b)}{c}\)

    2. Question 1a

    If \(p = 7\)
    \(a = 16\)
    \(b = -5\)
    \(c = 3\)

    then \(p^2 - \dfrac{(a - b)}{c}\)

    \(\Rightarrow\) \(7^2 - \dfrac{(16 - (-5))}{3}\)

    \(\Rightarrow\) \(49 - \dfrac{(16 + 5)}{3}\)

    \(\Rightarrow\) \(49 - \dfrac{(21)}{3}\)

    \(\Rightarrow\) \(49 - 7\)

    \(\Rightarrow\) \(42\)

    \(\therefore\) \(p^2 - \frac{(a - b)}{c}\) is 42







  2. Solve the inequality \(5x - 3(x - 1) \geq 39\).
    Illustrate your answer on the number line.

    3. Question 1b

    \(5x - 3(x - 1) \geq 39\)

    \(\Rightarrow 5x - 3x + 3 \geq 39\)

    \(\Rightarrow 5x - 3x \geq 39 - 3\)

    \(\Rightarrow 2x \geq 36\)

    \(\Rightarrow \dfrac{2x}{2} \geq \dfrac{36}{2}\)

    \(\Rightarrow x \geq 18\)

    \(\therefore \underline{\{x:x \geq 18\}}\)









  3. If \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix}-3 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}\) and \(\mathbf{y} = \begin{pmatrix}4 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}\), find
    \((i)\) \(\mathbf{x} + 2\mathbf{y}\)
    \((ii)\) \(3\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y}\)

    4. Question 1.c.

    \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix}-3 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}\)

    \(\mathbf{y} = \begin{pmatrix}4 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}\)

    1.c.i.

    \(\mathbf{x} + 2\mathbf{y}\)

    \(\Rightarrow\) \(\begin{pmatrix}-3 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix} + 2\begin{pmatrix}4 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}\)

    \(\Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix}-3 + 2(4) \\ 2 + 2(-1) \end{pmatrix}\)

    \(\Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix}-3 + 8 \\ 2 - 2 \end{pmatrix}\)

    \(\Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}\)

    \(\therefore \underline{\mathbf{x} + 2\mathbf{y} \ \text{is} \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix} }\)



    1.c.ii.

    \(3\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y}\)

    \(\Rightarrow 3\begin{pmatrix}-3 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix} - \begin{pmatrix}4 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}\)

    \(\Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix}3(-3) - 4 \\ 3(2) - (-1) \end{pmatrix} \)

    \(\Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix}-9 - 4 \\ 6 + 1 \end{pmatrix} \)

    \(\Rightarrow \begin{pmatrix} -13 \\ 7 \end{pmatrix} \)

    \(\therefore \underline{3\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y} \ \text{is} \begin{pmatrix} -13 \\ 7 \end{pmatrix} }\)
