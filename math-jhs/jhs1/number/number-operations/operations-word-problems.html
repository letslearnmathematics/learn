Introduction

In this lesson, we will learn about some of the phrases or words that will require the use of the basic operators in mathematics.

The operators that we will be considering are addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.

Addition \((+)\)

Main Idea

Some of the phrases that will require the use of the addition operator are:

\(*\) plus

\(*\) add

\(*\) calculate the sum

\(*\) increase a number by

\(*\) find the total
