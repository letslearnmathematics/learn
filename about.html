Our Story

Empowering students through quality education since 2015

Our Mission

At The Let's Learn Project, we're dedicated to making quality education accessible to all students, regardless of their background or location. Our mission is to:

  • Provide comprehensive learning resources
  • Simplify complex concepts through clear explanations
  • Help students build confidence in their academic abilities
  • Prepare students for academic success in examinations

Our History

2015

Founded at Good Shepherd International School

Started as an after-school program to help students struggling with mathematics in New Gbawe Community, Accra.

2017

Expanded to Science Subjects

Recognizing the need for comprehensive STEM education, we added science resources to our curriculum.

2019

Launched Online Platform

Developed our digital presence to reach students beyond our immediate community.

2022

Added English & Computing

Expanded our offerings to include language arts and digital literacy programs.

2025

Serving Thousands of Students

Now helping students across Ghana and beyond with our comprehensive learning resources.
