Section B

Try the questions first, using not more than 15 minutes for each question, and watch the accompanying videos to see how the questions are solved.





Question 1


  1. Simplify \(2\frac{3}{4} \div (3\frac{3}{8} - 1\frac{1}{2})\)

  2. There are 50 pupils in a class. Out of this number, \(\frac{1}{10}\) speak French only and \(\frac{4}{5}\) of the remainder speak both French and English. If the rest speak English only,

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) find the number of students who speak

    \(\hspace{1cm} \alpha)\) both French and English
    \(\hspace{1cm} \beta)\) only English.

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) Draw a Venn diagram to illustrate the above information.





Question 2


    A shop sells a pencil at ₵500.00 and a pen at ₵2,000.00.

  1. If Afua bought 8 pencils and 5 pens, how much did she pay altogether for them?

  2. The price of a pencil is increased by 20% and a pen by 10%. Find how much she will pay for 10 pencils and 8 pens.





Question 3


  1. Using a ruler and a pair of compasses only, construct

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) triangle \(ABC\) such that \(|AB| = 12\) cm, \(|AC| = 8\) cm and \(BAC = 30^\circ\).

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) a perpendicular from \(C\) to meet \(AB\) at \(M\).

  2. Measure

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) angle \(ABC\)

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) \(|CM|\)

  3. Calculate the area of triangle \(ABC\).





Question 4


  1. Solve the inequality \(\frac{5x - 3}{6} - \frac{2x - 4}{4} < 2\)

  2. \(i)\) Copy and complete the table of values for the relation, \(y = 2x + 1\)

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) Using a scale of 2 cm to 1 unit on the \(x-\)axis and 2 cm to 2 units on the \(y-axis\), plot the ordered pairs \((x, y)\) on a graph sheet.

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} iii)\) Use a ruler to join the points plotted.

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} iv)\) Use your graph to find

    \(\hspace{1cm} \alpha)\) \(x\) when \(y = 4\)

    \(\hspace{1cm} \beta)\) \(y\) when \(x = -2.5\)





Question 5


    The table shows the number of students in JSS class who prepared various dishes for their practical.

  1. \(i)\) Draw a pie chart for the distribution above.

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) What dish was prepared most?

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} iii)\) What percentage of students prepared Fried Rice?

  2. What is the probability that a student chosen at random cooked Fried Plantain and Beans?
