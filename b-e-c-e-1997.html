Question 1


\(a)\) If \(p = 7, a = 16, b = -5\) and \(c = 3\),
\(\hspace{0.5cm}\) evaluate \(p^2 - \frac{(a - b)}{c}\)


\(b)\) Solve the inequality \(5x - 3(x - 1) \geq 39\).
\(\hspace{0.5cm}\) Illustrate your answer on the number line.


\(c)\) If \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix}-3 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}\) and \(\mathbf{y} = \begin{pmatrix}4 \\ -1 \end{pmatrix}\), find
\(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) \(\mathbf{x} + 2\mathbf{y}\)
\(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) \(3\mathbf{x} - \mathbf{y}\)





Question 2


\(a)\) Using a ruler and pair of compasses only:

\(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) Construct triangle \(ABC\) such that \(|AB| = 8\) cm, \(|BC| = 8\) cm and \(\angle BAC = 60^\circ\).

\(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) What type of triangle is triangle \(ABC\)?

\(b)\) Contruct the bisector of \(\angle BAC\) to meet \(BC\) at \(D\). Measure \(AD\).

\(c)\) Construct the perperdicular bisector of \(AB\) to meet \(AD\) at \(O\).

\(d)\) Using \(O\) as centre and radius \(OD\), draw a circle to touch the three sides of the triangle.





Question 3


\(a)\) \(2y - 5x + 10 = 0, find\)

\(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) \(y\), when \(x = 2\)

\(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) \(x\), when \(y = 5\)

\(b)\) Using a scale of 2 cm to 1 unit on both axes, draw two perpendicular lines \(Ox\) and \(Oy\) on a graph sheet.

\(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) On the same graph sheet mark the \(x-\)axis from \(-5\) to \(5\) and the \(y-\)axis from \(-6\) to \(6\).

\(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) Plot on the same graph sheet the points \(A(0, -5)\) and \(B(4, 5)\). Join \(AB\) using a ruler.

\(\hspace{0.5cm} iii)\) Find the gradient of the line \(AB\).

\(\hspace{0.5cm} iv)\) Measure the acute angle the line \(AB\) makes with the \(x-\)axis, using a protractor.





Question 4


\(a)\) The table below shows the distribution of the ages of children who were treated in a clinic in a day.

Find

\(\hspace{0.5cm} i)\) the mean age,

\(\hspace{0.5cm} ii)\) the modal age.

\(b)\) Draw a bar chart for the distribution.





Question 5


\(a)\) The volume of a cylinder is \(220\) cm\(^3\), the radius of the cross section is 2.5 cm. Find the height of the cylinder. [Take \(\pi = \frac{22}{7}\)]

\(b)\) Each of the interior angles of a regular polygon is \(140^\circ\). How many sides has it?
