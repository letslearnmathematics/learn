Using Multi-base Blocks

Let's first look at the various components that we will need in building our model, which are:

  • a cube
  • a rod
  • a flat
  • a block
    •

    It can be seen from the diagram above that 1 rod can be created by stacking 10 cubes together.

    Also, 10 rods together make a flat.

    And, 10 flats together make a block.


    It implies that:
    1. 10 cubes make 1 rod.
    2. 100 cubes make 1 flat.
    3. 1000 cubes make 1 block


    Also,
    1. 10 rods make 1 flat.
    2. 100 rods make 1 block.


    And as said early on, 10 flats make 1 block.
