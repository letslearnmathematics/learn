Frequency Tables


In our previous lesson, we learnt how to use tallies to make records of various occurrences in a raw data.

We used vertical marks to represent each occurrence in the raw data.

In this lesson, we will learn about how to organise those tally records into a frequency table, also known as a frequency distribution.

Frequency implies the number of times a particular record appears in a raw data.
