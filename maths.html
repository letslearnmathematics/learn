MATHEMATICS

Let's Learn Mathematics started as a project to help the Basic 7 to 9 students of the Good Shepherd International School, located in the New Gbawe Community, Accra, to get used to solving questions.

The hope is that, once you go through all the topics on this website and practice the accompanying questions, you should find yourself well prepared to sit and pass the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE), and the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).
