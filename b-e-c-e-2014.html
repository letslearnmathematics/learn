Question 4


  1. A box has length 8.0 cm, width 5.0 cm and height 10.0 cm. Find the

  2. \(i)\) Using a scale of 2 cm to 1 unit on both axes, draw two perpendicular axes \(Ox\) and \(Oy\) on a graph sheet.

    \(\hspace{0.5cm} v)\) Draw the image \(A_2B_2C_2\) of triangle \(ABC\) under reflection in the \(x\)-axis.
