Question 1

a) List the members of each of the sets
\(\hspace{0.6cm} B=\){Whole numbers from 20 to 30} and
\(\hspace{0.6cm} D=\){factors of 63}
\(\hspace{0.6cm}\)List the members of:
\(\hspace{0.6cm}i)\) \(B \cap D\)
\(\hspace{0.6cm}ii)\) \(B \cup D\)



b) In a class of 60 students, 46 passed Mathematics and 42 passed English Language. Every student passed at least one of the two subjects.

\(i)\) Illustrate the information on a Venn Diagram.

\(ii)\) How many students passed in both subjects?

Let \(n\) represent the number of students who passed in both subjects.





Question 2


a) Factorise completely:

\(\hspace{0.7cm} 3a^2 + 2ab - 12ac - 8bc\)

b) Solve \(\frac{x}{4} + \frac{3}{5} = \frac{3x}{2} - 2\)

c) Find the solution set of \(x + 3 > 19 - 3x\), where \(x\) is a real number.
\(\hspace{0.5cm}\) Illustrate your answer on the number line.





Question 3


a) Using a ruler and a pair of compasses only:

\(\hspace{0.6cm}i)\) Contruct a triangle \(PQR\) such that \(|PQ| = 6cm\), \(|QR| = 4cm\) and \(\angle PQR=90°\).

\(\hspace{0.6cm}ii)\) Contruct perpendicular bisectors (mediatos) of \(PQ\) and \(QR\). Name the intersection of the mediators \(O\)

\(\hspace{0.6cm}iii)\) Draw a circle with \(O\) as center and \(OQ\) as radius.


b) Measure

\(\hspace{0.6cm}i)\) \(\lvert PR \rvert \)

\(\hspace{0.6cm}ii)\) \(\angle QPR\)





Question 4


The following is the result of a survey conducted in a class of a Junior Secondary School to find the favourite soft drink of each pupil in the class.

a) Draw a bar chart showing this information, using a scale of 2cm to 1 unit on the vertical axis.

b) How many pupils are in the class?

c) What percentage of pupils in the class prefer Club Cola?
